The Groom

Born on May 6, 2000, in Manila, Philippines, our groom is a dedicated software engineer whose passion for innovation shines through in every project he undertakes. Originally from Metro Manila, he embraced new beginnings by relocating to Liloan, Cebu, where he now shares a joyful home with his beloved bride. Over the past year, their journey together has been filled with love, laughter, and countless memorable moments that have laid the foundation for a lifetime of shared dreams and adventures.

Story

Our Love Story

First Meet

June 2015

Although they both grew up in the same community and attended the same elementary school, fate had its own plan. It wasn’t until 9th grade that their paths finally crossed, sparking a love story that has been unfolding ever since.

Friends to Lovers

August 2015

The groom and bride began as friends, often paired together for school assignments. What started as simple group work soon evolved into after-school hangouts and late-night chats on social media. As they got to know each other better, their bond blossomed into something truly special.

First Date

October 8, 2016

The bride and groom’s first date was at Amo Yamie Crib in Mendiola, Manila—sharing frappes and delicious bites as they got to know each other. Although that beloved spot has since closed its doors, the memories made there still open a special place in their hearts.

First Anniversary

February 8, 2017

For their first anniversary, the bride and groom enjoyed a relaxed after-school hangout. They watched The Space Between Us at SM Manila and strolled through Rizal Park in matching shirts. As the golden hour set in, the bride surprised the groom with a self-made album filled with their favorite memories and a heartfelt message.

Last Date, New Beginnings

April 10, 2017

On their final day of school, the bride and groom shared a bittersweet afternoon at Mall of Asia. They rode the Ferris Wheel, watched the sunset, and enjoyed a meal by the shore—a poignant farewell before the bride’s permanent move to Cebu. Though parting was painful, their hearts carried the hope that destiny would bring them back together.

Apart, But Always Together

2017 to 2022

Though separated for five years, their love never wavered. Even when falling asleep with active video calls, they found joy in celebrating every monthsary and anniversary—dining together virtually, exchanging thoughtful gifts, streaming movies, playing online games, and sharing countless chats on social media. Their story is a shining example that distance only deepens the bond of true love.

Next Date, In Each Other’s Arms

April 8, 2022

After the lockdown, the bride flew back to Manila so they could share some much-needed time together. Their first adventure took them to Tagaytay, where they explored charming attractions and savored the cool, refreshing weather. They strolled the historic streets of Intramuros, spent an exhilarating day at the mall enjoying arcades and delightful dining experiences, and even bonded with the groom's family over ice skating and bowling. As the trip came to an end, the groom walked the bride to the airport, cherishing every goodbye until they meet again.

Traveling Together, Growing Together

2022 to 2024

For the past two years, despite living in Cebu and Manila respectively, the bride and groom have cherished every moment together—traveling to each other's hometowns, exploring each city's hidden treasures, and joyfully meeting the families who would soon become one.

Our Forever Starts Here

January 2024

After five years apart, the groom chose to join the bride in Cebu, and together they embarked on countless new adventures that only deepened their bond. In October 2025, they joyfully discovered that their family was about to grow with the most beautiful surprise—a new life on the way.
